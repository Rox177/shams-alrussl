Our Method

The Construction Process

How we take your project from an initial concept to structural delivery.

01

Consultation

Discuss requirements, project scale, expectations, and initial budget estimations.

02

Site Inspection

Perform site dimensions verification, soil assessments, and accessibility checks.

03

Planning

Build the project timeline, milestones tracking, and prepare the detailed BOQ.

04

Engineering Design

Finalize architectural layouts, structural designs, and MEP schematic approvals.

05

Construction

Execution of civil, structural frame, brickworks, utilities piping, and finishes.

06

Quality Inspection

Rigorous verification of systems, finishes, leveling, and waterproof parameters.

07

Project Delivery

Handover of keys, warranties, structural drawings, and final client sign-off.
